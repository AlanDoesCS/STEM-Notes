## Power Formula

$$
\boxed{A^n = PD^nP^{-1}}
$$
where $A$ is a diagonalisable square matrix, $n \in \mathbb{N}$, $P$ is the matrix of eigenvectors of $A$, and $D$ is the diagonal matrix of the corresponding eigenvalues
